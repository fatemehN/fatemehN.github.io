Fatemeh N. Nokabadi


I am a PHD candidate at Université Laval and Mila working on object trackers and robustness of learning-based trackers. Since May 2023, I have pursued my Ph.D. program on the robustness of trackers, supervised by Professor Christian Gagné and Professor Jean-François Lalonde. I have published my research in Transactions on Machine Learning Research (TMLR) and presented at an ICML workshop, showcasing my work to the academic community.

Prior to this, I researched single object tracking using deep reinforcement learning algorithms until April 2023. Also, I have gained nine months of industry experience through internships, reinforcing my skills and learning from professional teammates.

For any inquiries, feel free to reach out to me via mail!
